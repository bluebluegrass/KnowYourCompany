Platform Regulatory Action

February 22, 2024: The WeChat mini-program 一舟一课 was suspended by Tencent/WeChat for violating the Interim Regulations on the Management of Public Information Services via Instant Communication Tools (《即时通信工具公众信息服务发展管理暂行规定》). This was a formal platform-level regulatory enforcement, not merely a terms-of-service removal. [translated from Chinese — source: 澎湃新闻, 每经网]

Simultaneously, Li Yizhou's personal WeChat Video Account was banned from accepting new followers, and his course listings were removed from all WeChat storefront pages. His Douyin (TikTok China) account became unsearchable by May 2024.

  • 《消费者权益保护法》(Consumer Protection Law) — inadequate refund process and misleading product descriptions

What It Is

一舟一课 (roughly: "One Boat, One Course") is the name of a WeChat mini-program (微信小程序) created by social media influencer Li Yizhou (李一舟) in 2023. It served as the sales channel for his AI course products, primarily targeting people with no technical background who wanted to learn about AI during the ChatGPT hype cycle.

It is not a registered company, not a startup, and not a conventional employer. It operates (or rather, operated) as part of Li Yizhou's personal content business, alongside his Douyin and WeChat Video Account channels.

Key Milestones

  • 2023: Li Yizhou launches "每个人的人工智能课" (AI for Everyone) at ¥199 RMB via WeChat mini-program 一舟一课. Upsell tiers added at ¥999, ¥1,980, and ¥2,980 RMB.
  • 2023–2024: Course sells ~250,000 units. Li Yizhou gains 3.31 million Douyin followers. A viral image pairing him with OpenAI's Sam Altman triggers mass public scrutiny.
  • February 22, 2024: WeChat mini-program suspended for regulatory violations. Personal video account banned from new followers. All course listings removed across platforms.
  • May 2024: Douyin account becomes unsearchable.
  • August 2024: Re-emerges on WeChat Video Account as "李一舟AI," selling a ¥99 RMB "one-person business" course.
  • March 2025: Resumes live streaming on video channels.
