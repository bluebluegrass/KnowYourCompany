11. Company Profile & History No concerns
▼

What They Do

Darktrace is a British AI cybersecurity company founded in 2013 in Cambridge, UK, by mathematicians and intelligence experts including Poppy Gustafsson, Jack Stockdale, Nicole Eagan, Dave Palmer, and Emily Orton. It was backed from inception by Invoke Capital, Mike Lynch's venture fund.

The company applies unsupervised machine learning to detect and autonomously respond to cyber threats in real time. It operates across network, email, cloud, endpoint, and operational technology (OT) security. As of 2023 it had approximately 2,269 employees across 100+ countries.

In the Netherlands, Darktrace has an Amsterdam office (sales/go-to-market) and a The Hague R&D centre, the latter inherited from its €47.5M acquisition of Dutch company Cybersprint in 2022.
